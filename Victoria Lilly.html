NORTH EAST

Wedding Photography

I'm Victoria Lilly, a professional North East photographer based in the North East. Specialising in creative, timeless and relaxed photography, I offer documentary style wedding photography services across the whole of the UK. To me, weddings are about the celebration of two people and two families coming together to create a special and unforgettable day. My passion for photography lies in my belief that there is nothing more exciting than capturing the memories that two people will treasure forever.

ABOUT

Victoria Lilly

I'm Victoria but known as Lilly to my family and I am a North East wedding photographer, who is based in Consett. Creating an insight into your wedding, the emotions and the details of the day, my relaxed photography style provides access to storyline that can be passed down from generation to generation.

When I am not on the school run or photographing, you will find me on the beach... and yep I generally have my camera there as well! As a family we love the great outdoors, if we're not outt walking we will be in the garden firing up the bbq and when I say we, I mean... my husband cooks and I drink rose...
